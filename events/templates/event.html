Innovate or Die? Driving Innovation in business

Join our inaugural meet-up to hear from leading figures on how their organisations are driving innovation and take some time to network with fellow thought leaders.

Register

Event

Driving innovations is critical in today’s business and an ever growing concern for businesses. To be competitive in the global marketplace, organizations need to be driving more innovation in their products and services. They need to innovate rapidly and they need to do it cost-effectively. Even the best technology can’t deliver success without focus on business strategy and goals. The first HackLaunch event will focus on addressing how to drive innovation, create an entrepreneurial and innovative culture that is for sustainable growth.

Innovation has nothing to do with how many R&D dollars you have. When Apple came up with the Mac, IBM was spending at least 100 times more on R&D than Apple. It's not about money. It's about the people you have, how you're led, and how much you get it.

Steve Jobs - Apple CEO
